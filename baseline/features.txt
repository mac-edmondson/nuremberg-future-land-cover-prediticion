    B2,B3,B4,B8 -> 10m resolution
    B5, B6, B7, B8A, B11, B12 -> 20m resolution
    B1, B9 -> 60m resolution

01. NDVI(vegetation)
It detetcts the vegetation health

NDVI = (B8 - B4) / (B8 + B4)

Here,
-High NDVI → vegetation
-Low NDVI → buildings, soil, water

Here the NDVI values defines the type of vegetation in the grid
~0.6–1	-->     Dense vegetation
~0.2–0.5 -->	Grass / sparse veg
~0–0.2 -->	    Built-up / soil
< 0	-->         Water




02. NDBI(Built-up Index)

NDBI = (B11 - B8) / (B11 + B8)

- B11 - 20m resolution - Reflects built-up areas
- B8 - 10m resolution  - Vegetation reflection

- Resample B11 from 20m to 10m

Here,
- High NDBI → urban
- Low NDBI → vegetation

Helps detect:
- urban expansion
- densification


03. NDWI (Water Index)

NDWI = (B3 - B8) / (B3 + B8)

Both bands are 10m resolution

Here,
- High NDWI → water


04. SAVI (Soil Adjusted Vegetation Index)

SAVI = ((B8 - B4) / (B8 + B4 + L) ) * (1 + L)

- Where L is soil adjustment factor/ stabilization factor (hyperparameter)
- L = 0.5

- B8 and B4 both are 10m resolution bands

Here,
SAVI reduces the soil brightness effect because NDVI says its vegetation or not

- High L --> more soil correction 
- Low L --> closer to NDVI

SAVI value	-->     Meaning
~0.6–1	    -->     dense vegetation
~0.2–0.5	-->     sparse vegetation
~0–0.2	    -->     bare soil / built-up
< 0	        -->     water

Helps in urban + vegetation change




05. delta_ndvi(change b/w 2020 and 2021 or more)

delta_ndvi = ndvi_2021 - ndvi_2020

Value	    -->     Meaning
~Positive	-->     vegetation increased
~Negative	-->     vegetation decreased
~ 0         -->	    stable




06. delta_ndbi(change in built-up intensity b/w 2020 and 2021 or more)

delta_ndbi = ndbi_2021 - ndbi_2020

Value	    -->     Meaning
~Positive	-->     vegetation increased
~Negative	-->     vegetation decreased
~ 0         -->	    stable




07. delta_ndwi(change in water/moisture b/w 2020 and 2021 or more)

delta_ndwi = ndwi_2021 - ndwi_2020

Value	    -->     Meaning
~Positive	-->     vegetation increased
~Negative	-->     vegetation decreased
